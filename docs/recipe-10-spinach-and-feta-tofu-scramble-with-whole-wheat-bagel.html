Day 10

Spinach and Feta Tofu Scramble with Whole Wheat Bagel

A cooked breakfast with step-by-step heat control and sequencing notes so each ingredient finishes at the right time.

Serves 1 Method: skillet Tags: whole-grains, produce, protein

Beginner-friendly instructions

  1. Heat the skillet over medium heat before adding oil. A warmed pan helps food cook evenly.
  2. Add sturdy ingredients first, such as onion, mushrooms, or sweet potato, because they take longer to soften.
  3. When vegetables are partly cooked, add quicker ingredients such as spinach, cooked beans, tofu, or eggs.
  4. Stir often enough to prevent burning, but not constantly. Letting food sit for a moment can improve browning and flavor.
  5. Taste a small bite before serving and adjust with salt, pepper, or other seasonings.

Ingredients

  • 0.5 block firm tofu
  • 1 cup baby spinach
  • 1 tbsp low-fat feta
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 1 small whole-wheat bagel

Equipment

  • skillet
  • spatula
  • bowl
  • fork

Before you start

  • Set out your equipment first: skillet, spatula, bowl, fork.
  • Read every step one time before you turn on the heat so nothing feels rushed.
  • Wash produce, dry it if needed, and measure ingredients into small bowls or cups before cooking.

Ingredient notes

  • If the tofu looks very wet, wrap it in paper towels and gently press for 5 to 10 minutes so the final dish tastes less watery.
  • Cut vegetables into small, similar pieces so they soften at the same speed and are easier to chew in a breakfast dish.

Original recipe card summary

  1. Crumble tofu and saute in oil.
  2. Add spinach until wilted; stir in feta.
  3. Serve with toasted bagel.

If something goes wrong

  • Not flavorful enough: add a pinch of salt, a squeeze of lemon, or a dusting of your favorite spice blend.

Make-ahead and leftovers

Most cooked grains, beans, and chopped fruit can be prepared ahead. Store leftovers in covered containers. If texture changes after chilling, add a splash of water or milk and warm or stir again before eating.
